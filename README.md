## More about me...

My academic background enables me to understand and work with low-level languages, such as Assembly. I have extensive proficiency in C, along with working knowledge of C++ and Python. I am open to collaborations and new project opportunities, particularly those involving complex technical challenges that push the boundaries of my current expertise.
